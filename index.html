Welcome to my reading corner

“The more that you read, the more things you will know. The more that you learn, the more places you’ll go.” – Dr. Seuss

Hi guys welcome to my book website! I just wanna start to read more, so I might say that actually I haven't read that lot of books. I made this website to help and motivate me to read more. Hope it can be helpful for you too. I realize that reading as part of the literacy is very important, but many of us may still often underestimate that literacy movement. Meanwhile most of us also know the statement that "Book is a window to the world" right? So I think we should have known why reading is that important. One of the basic reasons why reading is important is that it helps you grow mentally, emotionally and psychologically. Reading can help develops and expands your mind through the new knowledge that you get. Every book gives you an opportunity to learn new things and explore new ideas.
